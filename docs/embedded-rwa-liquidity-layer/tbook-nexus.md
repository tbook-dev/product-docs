# TBook Nexus

## What is TBook Nexus

TBook Nexus is TBook’s incentive campaigns and incentive assets management platform. It consists of three components:

* Verifiable soulbound tokens (vSBTs) that serve as Proof of Contribution or Credentials. vSBTs are a unique standard to TBook, enabling portable, tamper-proof contribution credentials and reputation across ecosystems;&#x20;
* A partner-facing portal that allows projects to issue vSBTs tied to a programmable range of user actions (e.g. staking, swapping or even joining a Discord AMA);
* An attestation network that cross-checks the validity of user contributions and credentials, enabling users to mint vSBTs once their actions are verified. Click here to see a sample user vSBT claim flow.

## Stackable Contributions&#x20;

Users contribute in different ways. Some deploy capital into DeFi protocols, some play on-chain games; a user may retweet a post, vote in a DAO or join a Discord AMA. TBook Nexus helps projects make sense of complex incentive scenarios, by creating vSBTs track which users made contributions, how they contributed and attributing value to their contributions. vSBTs serve as immutable on-chain Proof of Contribution or Credentials that can be easily programmed into smart contract logic that powers token distributions—airdrops, tokenized payouts, or RWA yield.

## Versatile Incentive Assets

TBook Nexus allows projects to distribute both on-chain and off-chain assets — including tokens, NFTs, SBTs, points, and whitelists — giving them complete flexibility in how they reward users. This versatility makes TBook Nexus suitable for a wide range of companies. For example, a Web3 project might airdrop NFTs alongside tokens at TGE, or reward early contributors with points and whitelists pre-launch. A Web2 company, on the other hand, can distribute stablecoin payments to KOLs and contractors across borders, as salaries or bonuses tied to specific KPIs.&#x20;

With TBook Nexus, projects can design incentive assets with limitless customization, opening new space for innovation.

## Flexible Asset Distribution

TBook Nexus provides a full set of incentive asset distribution methods for projects, including first-come-first-served, random allocation, equal distribution, layered distribution etc. Distributions can also be conditionally mapped to vSBTs or TBook’s WISE Credit Score.

TBook Nexus is designed to be flexible. At different stages of growth, faced with different needs, projects can execute high frequency, small volume and customized incentive programs, or launch a one-time, million-user scale community airdrop.

## Real-Time Intelligence

TBook Nexus equips projects with real-time intelligence, offering a comprehensive view of campaign performance, user engagement, as well as the status of incentive asset claiming. This enables projects to make data-driven decisions to optimize their incentive strategies.

Insights that can be tracked include:

* Live User Participation
* User Profiling
* Incentive Assets Distribution and Claiming

With TBook Nexus real-time intelligence, projects can continuously evaluate and refine their incentive strategies. Real-time intelligence enables projects to effectively engage core community members, and execute incentive programs with higher capital efficiency.
