# Artela

Artela's integration of TBook is a typical use case showcasing how a layer 1 blockchain leverages TBook's incentive layer to improve their overall incentive efficiency. &#x20;

Artela is the first extensible Layer 1 blockchain with parallel execution and modular VMs. By partnering with Artela, TBook improves its the incentive efficiency.

As a modular blockchain incentive layer, TBook provides rich incentive tools, meeting complex incentive demands of public blockchains, improving their overall incentive efficiency.

## Boosting Artela's Incentive Efficiency

### Integrating Incentive Passport

Incentive Passport is a universal multidimensional identity hub for managing incentive footprint and incentive assets. Incentive Passports streamlines the process for users to participate and engage in community activities.

Artela has integrated TBook's Incentive Passport in the Artela's Renaissance Program. Incentive Passports helps users easily participate in Artela tasks. Users can easily make contributions to Artela ecosystem, and manage different types of incentive assets.

Incentive Passports helps boost Artela's overall on-chain engagement, empowering projects to accurately target their incentive audience. This fuels the rapid improvement of the blockchain's overall incentive efficiency.

### Community Contribution Insights

TBook's WISE Credit Score is a comprehensive index which measures users’ multidimensional index. WISE means Wealth, Identity, Social and Engagement.

Leveraging TBook's WISE Score, users' contributions to the Artela ecosystem can be measured, making it much easier to discover the core community, and execute accurate airdrops.

### Measuring Contributions with WISE Score&#x20;

WISE Credit Score empower users to showcase their web3 impact. For users, having higher WISE Credit will not only be an honor in the community, but also opens them up to more incentive opportunities.

With WISE Credit Score, Artela can execute better user segmentations, helping itself design better incentive strategies. Seeking a higher WISE Credit Score, the community of Artela is motivated to engage more, contribute more. With this positive feedback dynamics, TBook helped Artela to greatly boost its community engagement and contribution, with a much higher incentive efficiency.
