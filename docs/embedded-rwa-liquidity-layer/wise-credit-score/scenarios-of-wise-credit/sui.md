# Sui

TBook serves as the token distribution infrastructure for the Sui ecosystem. By integrating TBook, Sui projects significantly improve incentive efficiency while driving higher user engagement across the network.

Users can easily access their WISE Score with just a few clicks through Sui Wallet or OKX Wallet at [engage.tbook.com](http://engage.tbook.com). TBook has played a key role in driving the mass adoption of WISE Credit, enabling users to showcase their cross-chain, cross-platform, and multidimensional impact within Web3.

## Sui WISE Credit

WISE Score is a cross-chain, cross-platform, multidimensional index that digitizes a user’s impact across Web3. Users generate their WISE Score through the Incentive Passport, and top scorers are featured on the WISE Score Leaderboard.

The score is calculated across four dimensions: Wealth, Identity, Social impact, and Engagement. By making contribution and influence visible, WISE Credit motivates users to participate more actively and pursue higher impact. Higher scores unlock more opportunities, fostering a competitive and thriving community on Sui.

For projects, WISE Credit enables efficient user segmentation, precise incentive targeting, and long-term growth strategies. For users, credits accumulate over time, creating lasting value. This alignment delivers mutual benefits across the entire Sui ecosystem.

## Efficient Interest Matching

TBook’s StableFi infrastructure already reaches more than 4 million active users. Through TBook, users aggregate their on-chain, off-chain, and cross-chain identities, showcase their overall community impact, and discover new projects.

Two core tools power this matching engine:

* WISE Score Leaderboard – helps projects quickly identify the most valuable and influential users.
* Engagement Basecamp – allows users to explore new and trending assets on Sui through banners, featured campaigns, SBTs, and RWAs.

Together, these tools improve interest matching between projects and communities, driving more efficient distribution, engagement, and long-term ecosystem growth.
