# Deploy New SBT

{% stepper %}
{% step %}

### Enter SBT Info

Click `Deploy your first SBT` and fill in the SBT metadata as prompted on the page.

<figure><img src="/files/0PhZptlU2gMceCkOQJ0y" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% step %}

### Submit the SBT

Click `Deploy the SBT`, your SBT will be submit for review.

<figure><img src="/files/ZYruU1D1yPg1rU7iBdEq" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

After approved, you can view the SBT on explorer.
