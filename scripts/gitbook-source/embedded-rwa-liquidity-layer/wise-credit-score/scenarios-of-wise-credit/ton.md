# TON

TBook is granted by TON Foundation and is officially recognized as the incentive infrastructure for TON Ecosystem. The integration of TBook improves TON's overall incentive efficiency, while greatly boosting user engagement. Users can locate TBook on[ TON Community Official Page](https://ton.org/community-tools).

Users on Telegram can easily access TBook through its TMA. Users can access TBook's incentive paradigm with simple clicks on the TMA.

TBook also promoted its mass adoption of the WISE Credit Score, which allows users to showcase their **cross-chain,** **cross-platform** and **multidimensional impact.**

## TON WISE Credit

**WISE Score is a cross-chain, cross-platform, multidimensional index that digitizes users’ impact.**

Users can generate their WISE Score in the Incentive Passport, those with higher scores will be listed on the WISE Score Leaderboard.

WISE Score is users’ credit calculated based on users' Wealth, Identity, Social and Engagement.

The WISE Credit Score motivates users to actively participate and contribute, pursuing a higher impact. Higher credit exposes users to more opportunities, fostering a competitive and thriving community on TON.

Leveraging WISE Credit Scores, projects can efficiently segment users, recognize the best incentive targets, and design mechanisms that help with their long term success. Users can also accumulate their credits for their long term benefits. WISE Score brings mutual benefits for the entire TON ecosystem.

## Efficient Interest Matching

Tbook built its incentive infrastructure that reaches 900 million Telegram users. Through the TBook TMA, users can aggregate their on-chain, off-chain and cross-chain identities, showcase their overall community impact, and discover interesting projects. **WISE Credit Score Leaderboard** and **Incentive Hubs** are two key instrument to better match projects and community members.

**WISE Credit Score Leaderboard** is the key instrument that helps project quickly identify the best incentive targets.

**Incentive Hub** allows users to constantly explore new and interesting quests on TON, through operational formats like quest square banners, featured and trending quests recommendations, and themed quests.&#x20;
