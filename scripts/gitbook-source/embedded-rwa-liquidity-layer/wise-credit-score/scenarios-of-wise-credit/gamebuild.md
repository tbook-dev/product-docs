# GameBuild

The case of GameBuild showcases TBook's capability of handling complicated incentive scenarios for listed projects. GameBuild is merged from two projects that are already listed in major trading platforms.

## Efficient Core Community Revival

GameBuild encountered a major difficulty in reaching the core communities from both merging projects.

By integrating TBook's Incentive Layer, GameBuild was able to quickly identify its core community members. By designing and executing a series of incentive programs, TBook helped GameBuild boosted the community engagement.&#x20;

## Unparalleled Governance and Token Merger Strategies

The merging of Gamebuild required the agreement of the communities from both projects. During the preparations of the merger, all the on-chain governance was done through TBook. This includes multiple on-chain votings.

Leveraging Incentive Passports' data capabilities, both the merging projects was able to identify and activate their core community.

With TBook AI, GameBuild was able to make a series of adjustments to its tokenomics, reaching the ideal merger strategy which meets the demands of communities, investors and holders.

GameBuild team executed the a sequence of community voting and the voting results has been recognized by major exchanges. That's how GameBuild was born!

## Accurate Token Airdrops

The success of the GameBuild token merge has boosted the community confidence of the GameBuild community. But that was only the beginning!&#x20;

Leveraging TBook's Incentive Layer, GameBuild identified the most suitable community members, and executed a 5 million USD worth of airdrop to its core community. This motivated the community to contribute more.
