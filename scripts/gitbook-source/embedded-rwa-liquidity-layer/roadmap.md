# Roadmap

We are building the embedded RWA liquidity layer that brings institutional-grade tokenized yield into user-facing apps.  As the development team behind TBook, we are dedicated to creating and supporting networks, protocols, and dApps that unlock the intelligence and value of stablecoins and RWAs.\
The following details TBook's path to identity, intelligence, and smart settlement.
